    As a coach of RYAN Sport Club, I always understand the difficulties and the trainees have to go through. My own experience and practice, I have drawn a lot of things

    On the 14th and 15th of August, martial arts star Cung Le shared his experiences of self defense as well as defeating rival Michael Bisping to the Vietnamese NHM.

    Master Cung Le came to Vietnam to share his experiences in martial arts and rehearsals for the match against Michael Bisping during the UFC Fight Night on 23/8, many fans came to Saigon Sports Club, 514B Huynh Tan Phat, District 7 to witness the idol. Cung Le sincerely shared his experiences of martial arts career. He is quite fluent in Vietnamese and speaks with people more easily. He learned martial arts to protect himself when he was bullied by his friends and then made his name on the ring so his experience was very useful to martial artists. The martial arts star of Hollywood is also devoted to teaching moves to those who are passionate about martial arts, especially kickboxing, where he has won 17 games in his career. The match against Michael Bisping on August 23 in Macau is a big challenge for Cung Le. This martial artist is seven years older than Cung Le, taller and heavier. He was determined to beat Cung Le after dropping from 5th to 8th place on the UFC chart. Photo: Getty Images. In order to beat the opponent, Cung Le practiced very hard. He shared the secret of defeating his opponents: "Bisping is in very good form, in the fifth and in the first half. I do not have the specific skills to beat him but he will choose the situation and the chance to hit the game. knock out knock out ". The students enjoyed the sharing of Cung Le. These are precious experiences that they are not easy to get. A female fan did not hide the joy of meeting idols. Cung Lê ký tặng người hâm mộ.
