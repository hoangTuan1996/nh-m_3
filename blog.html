  • Become an excellent soccer player, easy or hard?

    Football is for everyone in the world, just you have endless passion

    Wanting to be a good player like Cristiano Ronaldo or Lionel Messi is not difficult. Only you seriously follow the 5 tips below.



    1. Apparently location

    Different positions require different training methods, which are shared by Mark Howard, physical head of Blackburn Rovers. "For example, a central midfielder often has to operate in a narrow range, requiring short, sudden turnovers. A winger needs broader handling situations, the ability to accelerate within 30 to 40 meters, and then slow down in position. A goalkeeper needs a good turn. "

    2. Remember to set the muscle groups behind
    Traumatic injuries are usually caused by an imbalance of the body. "When you do physical exercises, do not forget back exercises like back, back thighs or buttocks," Howard says.

    3. Drink a lot of water

    Keep your body hydrated to ensure it works best. "Just a little dehydration will affect performance," Howard said, "so drink plenty of water before and during exercise. For food, you should eat carbohydrate foods before the game for a few days to provide the energy needed for muscle. Do not eat on the day of competition, because it is too late.

    4. Stretching

    Flexibility is an important factor in preventing injury. "Before every game, we do a lot of stretching exercises according to the movements that may be used on the pitch. Pilates and yoga exercises are very useful for the player, it enhances muscular stability.

    5. Be quick

    Good strength and strength are important, "but we also need to make the body faster," Howard said. "Players need to accelerate better, stop and change direction without losing momentum. It depends a lot on the strength of the legs. "
